Use Dia to create everything from simple line drawing to complex flow charts and circut diagrams. It is vector-based, which means you can scale the image to any size without loss oif quality and objects can be moved and modified an unlimited number of times without degrading. Diagrams can be exported to EPS or SVG formats or printed directly from Dia. Large diagrams can be spread over multiple pages.

Dia contains a wealth of pre-defined symbols for creating UML diagrams, flowcharts, network diagrams, and electrical circuits. You can also create your own sheets of symbols. Also available on Linux.
